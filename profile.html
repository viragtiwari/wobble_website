Top 10 Places to Visit in Delhi NCR

Connaught Place (CP)

Connaught Place, commonly known as CP, is a bustling commercial and cultural hub in the heart of Delhi. It offers a vibrant atmosphere with trendy cafes, vibrant street performances, and a mix of historical charm and modern amenities.

Hauz Khas Village

Hauz Khas Village is a popular hangout spot known for its eclectic mix of cafes, bars, and boutiques. It also features a historic Hauz Khas Complex with medieval-era ruins and a serene lake.

Dilli Haat

Dilli Haat is a vibrant open-air market showcasing traditional handicrafts, textiles, and cuisine from different states of India. Visitors can explore unique cultural experiences and indulge in diverse food options here.

Lodhi Art District

The Lodhi Art District is an outdoor art gallery adorned with colorful murals and graffiti by renowned artists. It offers visitors a creative space to explore and appreciate contemporary art.

Kingdom of Dreams

Kingdom of Dreams is an entertainment complex in Gurgaon featuring live shows, musicals, and cultural performances. It offers immersive experiences that blend Indian mythology, arts, and entertainment.

Cyber Hub

Cyber Hub is a food and entertainment hub in Gurgaon known for its diverse dining options, lively ambiance, and occasional events. Visitors can enjoy a wide range of cuisines and socialize in this vibrant space.

Nehru Planetarium

The Nehru Planetarium offers interactive exhibits, astronomy shows, and stargazing sessions, providing visitors with an opportunity to explore the wonders of the universe in an engaging manner.

Indian Accent

Indian Accent is a renowned fine dining restaurant in Delhi known for its innovative Indian cuisine with a modern twist. It offers visitors a gastronomic experience blending traditional flavors with contemporary culinary techniques.

Ambience Mall

Ambience Mall in Gurgaon is one of the largest shopping malls in Delhi NCR, offering a wide range of retail outlets, entertainment options, and dining experiences. It's a popular destination for visitors to shop, dine, and unwind.
